About me

Born and raised in Denver, Colorado; I have a burning passion to learn. I dip my affection towards life into as many interests as I can which currently range from getting on the golf course and competing, to spending time on a snowboard, having an unhealthy obsession with Formula 1, all the way to simply teaching my dog new tricks.

My newest venture is the world of computer science. More so Web Development than anything else. I have been learning HTML5, CSS3, Javascript and all other things front-end. The world of coding intrigues me because there is endless amounts to learn, and ways to innovate.

For as long as I can remember i've had a keen interest into cameras. They tell a story only you can tell, and capture moments you'd never get back otherwise. My everchanging passions can always seem to clash with a camera which is why I have such a diverse portfolio. I am always up to a challenge and no matter what may put in front of me, I can guarantee i'll make it look good with a camera.
