FAQs

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand things happen. You can cancel or reschedule your shoot anytime before 7pm the day before the shoot without a fee. If you reschedule after 7pm the day before the shoot, or the day of the shoot, there is a 25% cancellation fee (of the total price excluding discounts/travel fees).

Do I need to be present at the time of the shoot?

No! As long as we have access to the property, there is no necessity for you to be on site at the time of the shoot.

Why do I need to pay for professional real estate photography when I can do it myself?

80% of buyers make their decision of photos and listings alone. And according to the Wall Street journal, listings with high quality photos can gain anywhere between $934 and $116,076 more on the market.

When will I receive the photos?

Your images will be sent to you within 24 hours of the shoot! Where you will be able to download them in your preferred format, as well as an optional marketing kit!
