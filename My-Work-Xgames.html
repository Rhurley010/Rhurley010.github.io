Project description

From a young age, I have been an avid attendee of the prestigious Winter X Games hosted in Aspen, Colorado. During a recent visit, I was able to capture stunning images of renowned athletes such as Scotty James, Danny Davis, & Chris Corning, among others. To show the shots, I produced a directors cut of a captivating lo-fi style video edit that serves as a fitting culmination of my time in Aspen.
